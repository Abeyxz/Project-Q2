Who Are We?

Shelter of Hope is a student-led advocacy that stands for compassion, responsibility, and respect for all living beings. We started this journey because we believe that every animal deserves a safe, loving home and a life free from cruelty or neglect. Our group was formed out of the desire to give a voice to those who cannot speak for themselves. By raising awareness, we hope to show others that protecting animals is a reflection of kindness in society. We are determined to make a change, starting from schools and even into families and local communities. Through our hard work efforts, we want to create a culture where animals are valued as part of our shared world.

Who Am I?

I am Abigail V. Catamora, a passionate student advocate who dreams for peaceful animal welfare. I have always believed that every animal deserves a safe, loving home and a life free from cruelty. Through Shelter of Hope, I dedicate my time to raise awareness, educate citizens, and support rescue and adoption of abandoned animals.
